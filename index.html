Core Interaction Film Festival

Core Interaction Film Festival is an international black&white short film festival based on New York City. A lot of producers around the world attend this event annually. There are also field for ammateur participants to get awards. The CIFF is open to everyone so now join our festival and explore the art of black&white film!!
